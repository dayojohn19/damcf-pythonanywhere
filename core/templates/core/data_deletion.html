Requesting Deletion of Your Data

At DAMCF REALTY & CONSULTANCY INC., we respect your right to have your personal data deleted. If you would like to request the deletion of your personal information, please follow the instructions below.

What Data We Collect

We may collect the following information about you:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Messages and inquiries submitted through our contact form
  • Property booking requests and viewing appointments
  • Facebook user ID (if you interact with our Facebook app)

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, please contact us using any of the following methods:

📧 Email Request

Send an email to: damcfrealtyinc@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Facebook Data Deletion

If you accessed our services through Facebook and want to delete data associated with our Facebook app:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings & Privacy
  2. Click on "Settings"
  3. Go to "Apps and Websites"
  4. Find "DAMCF REALTY" in the list
  5. Click "Remove" to revoke access and delete associated data

Alternatively, you can request deletion by contacting us directly using the methods above.

What Information We Need

To process your data deletion request, please provide:

  • Your full name
  • Email address or phone number you used to contact us
  • Any other information that helps us identify your records
  • Preferred method of confirmation (email or phone)

Processing Time

We will process your data deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation once your data has been deleted from our systems.

Data We May Retain

Please note that we may retain certain information if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Records required for tax or legal compliance
  • Information necessary to resolve disputes or enforce agreements
  • Backup copies for a limited period before permanent deletion
